
Code of Conduct
1. Purpose

Our goal is to maintain a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in this project.

2. Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

Treat others with respect and kindness.

Communicate constructively and professionally.

Be open to diverse opinions and experiences.

Support a collaborative and positive atmosphere.

3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments.

Personal attacks or insults.

Sharing private or confidential information.

Any form of hate speech or threatening behavior.

4. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and communication channels related to the project.
